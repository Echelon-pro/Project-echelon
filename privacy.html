Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

1.1 Purpose

This Privacy Policy explains how Project Echelon ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, protects, and shares your personal information.

1.2 Acceptance

By using Project Echelon, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Registration Information

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Password
  • Optional: Country, wallet address, personal bio

2.2 Technical Information

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Browser type
  • Access times
  • Platform interactions

2.3 Cookies and Tracking

  • Basic technical cookies
  • Session management
  • User experience optimization

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Purposes

  • User account management
  • Platform functionality
  • User authentication
  • Ranking system maintenance
  • Improving user experience

3.2 Communication

  • Account-related notifications
  • Platform updates
  • Support responses
  • Marketing communications (with consent)

3. Wallet Information and Transaction Data

3.1. When you use our platform, we collect and process the following wallet-related information:

  • Wallet type (limited to Coinbase Wallet, Trust Wallet, Binance Wallet, Exodus Wallet, Atomic Wallet, Ledger Live, or MyDoge Wallet)
  • Transaction amounts in DOGE
  • Transaction timestamps
  • Public wallet addresses

3.2. We do not store or have access to:

  • Private keys
  • Wallet passwords
  • Recovery phrases
  • Any other sensitive wallet credentials

3.3. All transactions must be conducted through the DOGECOIN NETWORK to ensure proper tracking and security.

4. Data Protection

4.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls
  • Secure data storage

4.2 Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request data deletion at any time.

5. Data Sharing

5.1 Third Parties

We may share data with:

  • Service providers
  • Analytics partners
  • Legal authorities when required

5.2 Data Transfers

Data may be processed in countries with different data protection standards. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

6. User Rights

6.1 Data Access and Control

Users can:

  • Access their personal data
  • Request data correction
  • Request data deletion
  • Export their data
  • Opt-out of marketing

6.2 Contact Us

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at info@project-echelon.com

7. Updates to Privacy Policy

7.1 Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be notified via email or platform announcement.

7.2 Version History
